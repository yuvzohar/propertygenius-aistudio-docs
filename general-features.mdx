### PropertyGenius AI Assistant Bot

The **PropertyGenius** **AI Assistant Bot** is available throughout the application to help answer questions, provide guidance, and perform actions - making your workflow faster and more efficient.

### Q&A Assistant

Ask any question related to the application and get instant clarification or guidance.

**Example:**<br />_"How can I switch between property phases?"_

### Actions Assistant

Ask the AI Assistant Bot to perform specific actions within the application.

**Example:**<br />_"Create a new task named 'Pay tax fee' for the 'New York Condo' property and add a reminder for Thursday, 16 July at 5:00 PM."_
