A quickstart should take someone from zero to using your product. They'll get a quick win and a sense of what they can accomplish.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, you must have:

- Requirement one (for example, Node.js 18\+, a free account, an API key)
- Requirement two (for example, a compatible device, a compatible browser, a compatible operating system)

## Get started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add a Property">
    Follow the instructions in the [Properties Inventory](/property-management) page to add your first Property item.<br /><br />

    <video src="/videos/add_property-1.mp4" controls />

    <br />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure & Manage">
    Click on the property item created in Step 1. <br />Based on the selected status phase, define the relevant milestones, tasks, and expenses.

    Once the **PLAN** and **WORK** phases are completed, or if the property was initially set to **READY**, you can define and manage your tenants. <br />Optionally, sync your Airbnb, [Booking.com](http://Booking.com), and Agoda calendars with the PropertyGenius calendar to keep your rental information **organized in one place**!

    <Frame caption="Defining Property Milestones">
      ![Property Milestones](/images/property_milestones.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Financial Perforance">
    Track your expenses, payouts, and financial performance using the Statistics charts available in each property's **Expenses** tab or through the main **Statistics** dashboard.

    ```bash
    your-cli start
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>
