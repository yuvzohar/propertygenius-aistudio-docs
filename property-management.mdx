The Property Inventory lets you create, organize, and manage all your properties in one place. It guides you through every stage of the property lifecycle - from planning and purchase, through renovation, to managing tenants for short-, mid-, and long-term rentals. With AI-powered tools and built-in integrations, it centralizes everything you need to manage your properties efficiently.<br /><br />

# Working with Property Item

Once you've created a property, you can begin managing it by adding milestones, tasks, expenses, tenants, and other related activities. Each property provides features and attributes tailored to its current lifecycle phase, helping you manage every stage efficiently.

## Property Lifecycle (Plan \\ Work \\ Ready)

Each property progresses through **three main lifecycle phases**:

- **PLAN** - Search for the right property, complete the purchase, sign contracts, and manage all planning activities, including legal, financial, tax, and regulatory processes.
- **WORK** - Manage renovations, construction, interior design, repairs, and any other work required to prepare the property for rental or sale.
- **READY** -  The property is fully prepared and ready to be rented or sold.<br />

<Frame>
  ![Property Phases](/images/property-phases.png)
</Frame>

<br />The initial lifecycle phase is selected when you create a property on the **Add Property** page. <br />If no phase is specified, the property is created in the **PLAN** phase by default.

You can update a property's lifecycle phase at any time by editing the property's details.<br /><br />

<Note>
  Note

  - When you've completed a phase, turn on the **Phase Completed** toggle to automatically advance the property to the next lifecycle phase.
  - To move the property back to the previous phase, simply turn off the **Phase Completed** toggle for the previous phase.<br />
</Note>
