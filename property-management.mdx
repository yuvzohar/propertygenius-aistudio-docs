The Property Inventory lets you create, organize, and manage all your properties in one place. It guides you through every stage of the property lifecycle - from planning and purchase, through renovation, to managing tenants for short-, mid-, and long-term rentals. With AI-powered tools and built-in integrations, it centralizes everything you need to manage your properties efficiently.<br /><br />

## Add Property<br />

Your journey starts by adding your properties.

<br />The following are the fields

- **Property Name\*** - Enter the name of you property
- **Property Profile** **Image** -Upload a representative image for your property. Supported formats: **.png** and **.jpg**. Maximum file size: **3 MB**. 
- **Location Details\* -** Enter the address of your property
- **Property Type\* -** This is an informational field only and does not affect your property's management, workflows, or calculations:  
  - APARTMENT (default) 
  - HOUSE
  - OFFICE
  - STORE
  - STORAGE
  - CONDO
  - LAND
- **Property Currency** - By default, all properties use USD as their primary currency. However, you can assign a different currency to each property individually. The selected currency will be used throughout the property's lifecycle, including all future transactions, reports, and analytical calculations.<br /><br />The supported values:
  - USD (default)
  - EUR
  - ILS
- **Current Status\* - PropertyGenius** helps you manage your property's entire lifecycle - from the initial planning stage, through renovation (if needed), to a property that's ready to sell or rent (short-, mid-, or long-term).  Phase status values:<br />
  - PLAN (default) - Planning and preparation before work begins.
  - WORK - Renovation, repairs, or other work is in progress.
  - READY - The property is ready to be sold or rented.<br />

<Info>
  **Note**<br /><br />Property phases do not change automatically. Once a phase is selected, you must manually mark it as complete before advancing to the next phase.<br />
</Info>

- **KeyBox 4-Digits PIN -** Store your property's 4-digit access code, such as a keybox PIN.<br />

\*mandatory fields<br />
