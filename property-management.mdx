The Property Inventory lets you create, organize, and manage all your properties in one place. It guides you through every stage of the property lifecycle - from planning and purchase, through renovation, to managing tenants for short-, mid-, and long-term rentals. With AI-powered tools and built-in integrations, it centralizes everything you need to manage your properties efficiently.<br /><br />

# Working with Property Item

Once you've created a property, you can begin managing it by adding milestones, tasks, expenses, tenants, and other related activities. Each property provides features and attributes tailored to its current lifecycle phase, helping you manage every stage efficiently.

## Property Lifecycle (Plan \\ Work \\ Ready)

Each property progresses through **three main lifecycle phases**:

- **PLAN** - Search for the right property, complete the purchase, sign contracts, and manage all planning activities, including legal, financial, tax, and regulatory processes.
- **WORK** - Manage renovations, construction, interior design, repairs, and any other work required to prepare the property for rental or sale.
- **READY** -  The property is fully prepared and ready to be rented or sold.<br />

<Frame>
  ![Property Phases](/images/property-phases.png)
</Frame>

<br />The initial lifecycle phase is selected when you create a property on the **Add Property** page. <br />If no phase is specified, the property is created in the **PLAN** phase by default.

You can update a property's lifecycle phase at any time by editing the property's details.<br /><br />

<Note>
  Note

  - When you've completed a phase, turn on the **Phase Completed** toggle to automatically advance the property to the next lifecycle phase.
  - To move the property back to the previous phase, simply turn off the **Phase Completed** toggle for the previous phase.<br />
</Note>

The initial lifecycle phase is selected when you create a property on the **Add Property** page. <br />If no phase is specified, the property is created in the **PLAN** phase by default.

#### Milestones

Define key milestones to stay organized and effectively manage your property's planning phase.

**Milestones Timeline**

The **Milestones Timeline** component provides a visual representation of your milestones by displaying them as markers along a timeline.

Hover over a milestone marker to view its name. Clicking a marker will automatically navigate you to the corresponding milestone in the list below. <br />

<Frame caption="Milestones Timeline">
  ![Milestones Widget](/images/milestones-widget.png)
</Frame>

<br />**Add Milestone**

Click **\+ Add Milestone** to create a new milestone.<br />The **Add Milestone** page includes the following fields and options:

- **Name** – Enter a meaningful name for the milestone.
- **Notes** – Add any relevant notes or details.
- **Date** – Set the milestone date.
- **Quick Attachments** – Attach related documents, images, tasks, and expenses.
- **Assigned Contacts** – Link contacts from the [**Contacts**](/contacts) page or create new ones directly from the milestone.<br />

In addition to milestone-specific tasks, the **PLAN** phase also lets you create property-level tasks from the **Tasks** tab.<br />

#### Add/Edit Task

A task includes the following fields and options:

- **Name** – Enter a descriptive name for the task.
- **Notes** – Add any relevant details or instructions.
- **Reminders** – Schedule reminders to help you stay on track. PropertyGenius can send notifications through multiple channels, including in-app alerts, platform notifications (such as your web browser or Android device), and email. Notification preferences can be customized. See [**Reminders**](/reminders) page to learn more.
- **Due Date** – Set the deadline for completing the task.
- **Task Status** – When the task is complete, turn on the **Completed** toggle to keep your work organized and up to date.<br /><br />

<Frame caption="Add/Edit Task">
  ![New Task](/images/new-task.png)

#### Expenses

The **Expenses** tab lets you manage all expenses and bills related to your property's **PLAN** phase in one place.

Like tasks, expenses created within a milestone are automatically displayed in the property's **Expenses** tab, giving you a complete view of all planning costs.

Each expense is recorded in the currency defined for the property and is automatically included in your financial analytics, including **Income & Expenses** and **Profit & Loss** reports. These reports can be exported or shared with your accountant whenever needed.<br />

<Frame caption="Planning Phase Expenses">
  ![Expenses Page](/images/expenses-page.png)
</Frame>

#### Add/Edit Expense

Each **Expense** item includes the following fields:

- **Type** – Select the expense type or service category.
- **Amount** – Enter the expense amount.
- **Date** – Specify the expense date.
- **Recurring Interval** – Choose how often the expense recurs: **One Time**, **Weekly**, **Monthly**, **Every 3 Months**, **Every 6 Months**, or **Yearly**.
- **Recurring Amount** – Enter the recurring payment amount (available for recurring expenses only).
- **Notes & Description** – Optionally add notes or additional details.
- **Reminders** – Set one or more payment reminders.
- **Mark as Paid** – Turn on this option once the expense has been paid.
- **File Attachments** – Attach invoices, receipts, or any other supporting documents.

<Frame caption="Add Expense">
  ![Add Expense Item](/images/add-expense-item.png)
</Frame>

#### Expenses Groups

To keep your expenses organized, PropertyGenius automatically groups expenses of the same type into a single parent item.

Click a parent item to expand it and view all of its individual expense entries.

#### Import Expenses from CSV

To save time, you can upload multiple existing expenses at once instead of adding each expense manually. This feature makes it quick and easy to import your expense records in bulk.<br />

<Frame>
  ![Expenses Import Csv 1](/images/expenses_import_csv-1.gif)
</Frame>

<br />The CSV file should include the following fields:

- **expense\_type**\* – The name of the service provider or supplier you paid. This value must already be defined under **Settings → Suppliers & Services**.
- **amount**\* – The expense or bill amount.
- **date**\* – The date of the expense or bill.
- **description** – Add any additional notes or details related to the expense.
- **url** – A URL link to a remote file or document attached to this expense item.
- **remind\_date** – Set a reminder date for the expense payment.
- **is\_paid** – Indicates the current payment status of the expense.

\*mandatory field

### Work Phase

The **Work phase** helps you organize all milestones, tasks, expenses, and contacts related to the execution stage of your property's lifecycle.

By toggling the **"Plan phase complete"** button, the property's status will automatically change to **WORK**, and you will be redirected to the property's Work phase page.

Alternatively, you can change the property's status at any time or set **WORK** as the initial status when creating the property.

Since the Work phase includes the same features and workflows described in the **PLAN phase** section, you can refer to the previous section for detailed information.<br />

<Frame caption="Work Phase">
  ![Work Phase](/images/work-phase.png)
</Frame>

### Ready Phase

While the **PLAN** and **WORK** phases are temporary stages - or may not be relevant for your specific use case - the **READY** phase is the long-term management phase for properties intended for rental (short-term or long-term).

The **READY** phase includes some features from the previous phases, along with additional tools and functionalities specifically designed for ongoing property management.<br />
