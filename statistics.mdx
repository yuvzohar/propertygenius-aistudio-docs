The Statistics page provides performance analytics and insights, helping you compare your portfolio with the average investor.<br />

## Statistics Dashboard

The **Statistics** dashboard provides a high-level overview of your portfolio's performance. <br />Filter the data by relevant segments to gain actionable insights and monitor key metrics, including:

- **Income, Revenue & Expenses** - Analyze your properties' financial performance over time.
- **Tenant Performance** - Track tenant activity, rental income, and payment trends.
- **Occupancy Rate** - Monitor occupancy levels and rental performance over time.
- **Ready Phase Insights** - Discover AI-generated insights for properties in the **Ready** phase to help optimize your investment decisions.<br />

<Frame caption="Statistics Dashboard">
  ![Statistics Dashboard](/images/statistics-dashboard.jpg)
</Frame>

<Note>
  **Currency Display**

  - If your properties use **different currencies**, and the **All Properties** filter is selected, all statistics are automatically converted and displayed in **USD (\$)**.
  - If **all properties use the same currency** (for example, **EUR**), all statistics will be displayed in that currency instead of USD.
  - When viewing a **specific property**, all statistics are displayed in the currency assigned to that property.
</Note>

## **Net Revenue Detailed Analysis**

Clicking on the 'Net Revenue' option on the Statistics Dashboard screen will navigate you to the 'Net Revenue Detailed Analysis' page.

<Frame caption="Net Revenue Detailed Analysis">
  ![Net Reveneu Details Analysis](/images/net-reveneu-details-analysis.jpg)
</Frame>

Review detailed analytical information related to your payouts, income and revenue.<br />Learn which property is the most profitable. <br />Which period was the most profitable.<br />Did you make more money from short term or long term?<br />Compare between external accomodation platforms you use - which provided more money?  

The **Net Revenue Detailed Analysis** page provides a comprehensive view of your rental income and property performance, helping you identify trends and maximize profitability.

Use this page to:

- Review detailed insights into your **income, payouts, and net revenue**.
- Identify your **highest-performing properties** based on generated revenue.
- Discover **which months or periods were the most profitable**.
- Compare the performance of **short-term and long-term rentals**.
- Analyze revenue generated from **different booking platforms** (such as Airbnb, Booking.com, Agoda, and others) to determine which channels deliver the highest returns.
- Filter and search revenue by **property, tenant, or contract** for more focused analysis.

The dashboard also provides valuable business insights, including:

- **Total Monthly Net Revenue** across your selected properties.
- **Active Rental Contracts** and the average monthly rent.
- Your **highest monthly rental income** and the property generating it.
- **Current Occupancy Rate** to help monitor portfolio utilization.
- **Monthly Revenue Trends** with interactive charts for identifying seasonal patterns, growth, or declining performance.
- **Revenue Distribution by Property**, allowing you to quickly compare how much each property contributes to your total income.
- A detailed **Rental Revenue Directory** showing each active tenancy, including:
  - Tenant information
  - Assigned property
  - Contract duration
  - Payment frequency
  - Contract status
  - Monthly rent amount

Additional capabilities include:

- Search and filter analytics by property or tenant.
- Export the displayed data to CSV for external reporting and accounting.
- Share a summarized revenue report with partners, investors, or team members.
- Monitor occupancy alongside revenue to understand whether vacancies are affecting profitability.
- Compare properties side by side to identify underperforming assets and opportunities for improvement.
- Track recurring versus one-time rental payments.
- Measure the financial contribution of each property to your overall portfolio, helping prioritize investments and renovation decisions.

This page enables you to make data-driven decisions about pricing, occupancy, marketing channels, and property investments, giving you a clear understanding of where your rental business is performing best and where improvements can increase revenue.
